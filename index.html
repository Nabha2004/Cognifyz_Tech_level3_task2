Web Developer Internship Opportunities at Cognifyz Technologies

Welcome to our exciting Web Development internship program! To complete this internship, you will have the chance to choose any 2 of 3 levels: Level 1, Level 2, or Level 3. We've designed these levels to cater to your convenience and ensure an engaging and rewarding experience. Additionally, the successful completion of Level 3 will further enhance your chances of receiving a stipend.

Highlights

  • Real-world experience and mentorship
  • Flexible hours per week
  • Remote work opportunities
  • Performance-based stipend up to 15,000 rupees

Qualifications

As a Developer at our organization, your expertise in developing and integrating technologies will help us build scalable solutions that meet customer needs. Your proficiency in front-end and back-end development is crucial for creating seamless user experiences and ensuring stability.

Internship Details

Position: Web Development Intern

Duration: 14/05/2024 to 14/06/2024 (Last Date of Task Submission)

Weekly Hours: Flexible hours per week

Location: Remote Work

Additional Information

  • Update your LinkedIn Profile and share all your achievements, tagging @Cognifyz Technologies and using #cognifyz #cognifyzTech #cognifyzTechnologies
  • If your project/code is found copied, your internship will be terminated.
  • Share a video of the completed task on LinkedIn and tag @Cognifyz Technologies.

After completing the first month of your internship, you will go through an interview process. If you pass, you will be eligible for a performance-based stipend ranging from 10,000 to 15,000 rupees.

If you wish to apply for the interview process, there will be a registration cost of 249/-. If you choose not to participate, you can pay 149/- to obtain your Internship Completion Letter and Recommendation Letter.
